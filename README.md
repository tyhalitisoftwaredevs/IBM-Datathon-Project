EcoCycle 
A smart, community‑driven recycling platform designed to promote environmental awareness, track waste habits and empower users to make sustainable choices.

About the Project
EcoCycle is built to encourage responsible waste management through simple digital tools. The platform helps users understand their recycling patterns, learn eco‑friendly habits and contribute to cleaner, greener communities. It blends technology, data insights and user engagement to turn everyday recycling into measurable impact.

Key Features
Waste Tracking: Monitor recycling habits and visualize progress over time.

Eco‑Education: Access tips, guides and resources on sustainable living.

Community Impact: See how individual actions contribute to collective environmental goals.

User‑Friendly Interface: Clean, intuitive design for all ages and backgrounds.

Why EcoCycle Matters
Recycling is more than a habit — it’s a lifestyle. EcoCycle empowers individuals and communities to take small steps that lead to big environmental change. By making sustainability accessible and engaging, the project supports a healthier planet for everyone.
